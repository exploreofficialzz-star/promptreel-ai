Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2025 · chAs Tech Group

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your email address, username, and video prompts you enter in the app. We also collect usage data such as features used, plans created, and session duration.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve PromptReel AI services
  • To process subscription payments via Flutterwave
  • To send service-related emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • To serve contextually relevant ads (Free plan users)

3. Advertising

Free plan users see ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings. On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency permission before serving personalised ads.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by Render (render.com) and a PostgreSQL database. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Payment Data

Payments are processed by Flutterwave. We do not store credit card numbers or full payment details on our servers. Flutterwave's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at privacy@chastechgroup.com.

7. Children's Privacy

PromptReel AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@chastechgroup.com
